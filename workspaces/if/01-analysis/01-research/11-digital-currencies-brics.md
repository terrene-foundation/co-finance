# Event 11: Digital Currencies, BRICS, and De-Dollarization

## CBDC Developments

### Digital Euro

- EU Council approved in December 2025; European Parliament expected to vote in first half of 2026
- If passed, ECB aims for testing mid-2027, potential first issuance by 2029
- Faces political headwinds — supporters are 40+ votes short of absolute majority

### China's Digital Yuan (e-CNY)

- **January 1, 2026**: Commercial banks began paying interest on digital yuan wallets — making e-CNY competitive with traditional deposits
- Digital yuan circulation rose 334% year-over-year

### US Position

- Trump's 2025 executive order halting retail CBDC development remains in effect
- US is the only major economy to explicitly block CBDC development
- Continues participating in wholesale cross-border research (Project Agora)

## BRICS Under India's 2026 Chairship

- **Theme**: "Building for Resilience, Innovation, Cooperation and Sustainability"
- **Membership**: Now 11 countries (~49.5% of global population, 40% of GDP, 26% of trade)
- **BRICS Unit**: 40% gold-backed, 60% BRICS currencies — pilot launched October 2025
- **Settlement**: BRICS nations now conduct ~90% of settlements in national currencies
- **Trade milestone**: BRICS trade flows expected to exceed G7 in 2026
- **CBDC interoperability**: Russia, China, India working to interlink digital ruble, yuan, rupee

## US Dollar Weakening

- **DXY**: Trading in high-90s, near multi-month lows
- **12-month decline**: Down 4.20% (down 9.4% in 2025 alone)
- **Paradox**: Tariffs were expected to strengthen the dollar; the opposite occurred because retaliation weakens it

## Discussion Angles

- Is de-dollarization real or overstated? What would it take for the dollar to lose reserve currency status?
- How do CBDCs change international finance? Compare the digital euro, digital yuan, and US CBDC refusal
- What is the "Triffin dilemma" and does BRICS settlement in local currencies help resolve it?
- How does the Iran war (Iran is now a BRICS member under US attack) test BRICS cohesion?
- What are the implications of BRICS trade exceeding G7 trade for the first time?
