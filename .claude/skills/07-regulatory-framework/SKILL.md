# Regulatory Framework for Financial Education

Compliance guidance, disclaimer templates, and regulatory rules for building financial education platforms that present market data, backtested results, or investment concepts.

## Overview

This skill covers regulatory considerations for financial education:

- SEC guidance on educational vs advisory content
- Ready-to-use disclaimer templates
- Data licensing and redistribution rules
- Hypothetical and backtested performance disclosure requirements

## Critical Disclaimer

This skill provides general educational guidance about financial regulations. It is not legal advice. Always consult qualified legal counsel for compliance decisions specific to your platform.

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when:

- Building a financial education platform or course
- Displaying market data, charts, or performance results
- Presenting backtested or simulated trading results
- Writing content that discusses investments or trading strategies
- Determining what data sources require licensing or attribution
- Drafting disclaimers for financial content
