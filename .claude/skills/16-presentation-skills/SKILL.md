# Presentation Skills

How to present financial analysis, research findings, and investment recommendations effectively. Covers slide design principles (including the assertion-evidence model), data visualization best practices for finance, narrative structure for financial presentations, and practical delivery techniques. Aimed at finance students preparing for class presentations, thesis defenses, case competitions, and job interviews.

## Quick Reference

1. **One Idea Per Slide** -- each slide should make exactly one point; the audience should grasp it in 3 seconds
2. **Assertion-Evidence Model** -- slide title states a full-sentence assertion; the body provides visual evidence
3. **Chart Selection** -- time series for trends, bar charts for comparisons, waterfall for composition changes, scatter for relationships
4. **SCR Framework** -- Situation (context) --> Complication (problem/opportunity) --> Resolution (your recommendation)
5. **Rule of Three** -- structure your presentation around three key messages; audiences remember three things
