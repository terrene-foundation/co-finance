# Personal Finance Fundamentals

Core personal finance concepts for financial education platforms, covering budgeting, tax-advantaged accounts, insurance, debt management, and the foundations of saving and investing.

## Overview

This skill covers the building blocks of personal financial literacy:

- Budgeting methods and cash flow analysis
- Tax-advantaged accounts (401(k), IRA, HSA, 529)
- Insurance types and debt management strategies
- Saving vs investing, compound growth, and asset allocation basics

## Educational Disclaimer

This content is for educational purposes only. It does not constitute financial, tax, or investment advice. Contribution limits, tax rules, and regulations change frequently. Always verify current figures with the IRS or a qualified professional.
