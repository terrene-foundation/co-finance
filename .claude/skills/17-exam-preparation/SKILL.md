# Exam Preparation

Evidence-based study techniques and exam strategies specifically tailored for finance courses. Covers how to study effectively using active recall and spaced repetition, how to approach different types of exam questions (calculations, conceptual, case analysis, essays), and how to create formula sheets that actually help during exams. Aimed at UG/PG students preparing for midterms, finals, and professional certification exams.

## Quick Reference

1. **Active Recall** -- test yourself on material rather than re-reading; retrieval strengthens memory far more than passive review
2. **Spaced Repetition** -- review material at increasing intervals (1 day, 3 days, 7 days, 14 days); fights the forgetting curve
3. **Feynman Technique** -- explain a concept in plain language as if teaching someone else; gaps in your explanation reveal gaps in understanding
4. **For Calculations** -- always state the formula, define variables, show substitutions, compute, interpret the result
5. **Formula Sheets** -- group formulas by topic, show the relationships between them, include units and sign conventions
