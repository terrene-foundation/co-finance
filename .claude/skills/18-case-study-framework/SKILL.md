# Case Study Framework

A structured approach to analyzing business and finance case studies. Covers the Harvard case method (the dominant approach in business education), the most commonly used analytical frameworks, a step-by-step DCF walkthrough for valuation cases, and a template for writing up case analyses. Essential for students in case-based courses, case competitions, and investment banking interviews.

## Quick Reference

1. **Harvard Case Method**: Read (identify facts) --> Analyze (apply frameworks) --> Discuss (defend your recommendation)
2. **SWOT**: Strengths, Weaknesses (internal) / Opportunities, Threats (external) -- quick situational snapshot
3. **Porter's Five Forces**: Rivalry, new entrants, substitutes, buyer power, supplier power -- industry attractiveness
4. **DCF Core**: Enterprise Value = PV of projected free cash flows + PV of terminal value
5. **Recommendation Test**: Is it specific, actionable, supported by evidence, and does it address the core problem?
